Benbulben

A hard and resistant limestone forms the upper cliffs and precipices. Benbulben was formed during the Ice age, when large parts of the earth were under glaciers. It was originally merely a large ridge, however the moving glaciers cut into the earth, leaving a distinct formation, now called Benbulben

Rossnowlagh

Rossnowlagh beach is located approximately 8 kilometres south of Donegal Town, and has soft, golden sand and exceptional views, Rossnowlagh is very popular with both local residents and tourists.The surf here is perfect for beginners and the local surf school offers tuition and equipment rental. As well as surfers, this beach also attracts walkers, swimmers and sailors. This beach has received the International Blue Flag award for 2019.

Fanad

Fanad Head Lighthouse is situated on the northern coast of the Fanad Peninsula in North Donegal. This light is classified as a sea light as distinct from a harbour light although it does mark the entrance to Lough Swilly which is a natural harbour of refuge
